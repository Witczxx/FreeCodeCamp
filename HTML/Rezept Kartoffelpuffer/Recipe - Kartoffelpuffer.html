A Recipe for Kartoffelpuffer

Today you are going to learn how to prepare Kartoffelpuffer by yourself.

Kartoffelpuffer are a famous german snack made out of potatoes.

There are a lot of, where people add different things as "dips" to eat with Kartoffelpuffer.

Ingredients

  • 500g Kartoffeln
  • 4 Eier
  • 250g Mehl
  • 500g Apfelmuß
  • Salz
  • Pfeffer

Instructions

  1. Cut the Kartoffeln into sm@allest pieces
  2. Add eggs and mix it well.
  3. Add Mehl and mix it well.
  4. Add Salz and Pfeffer according to your preference
